10 Most Popular Carpet Designs of 2023

  • 23 April 2024
  • 6 Min Read
  • By Jaipur Rugs

Jaipur Rugs reveals its most sought-after carpet designs of this year

Carpets can do a multifold of things. They bring order to a room full of disarray by anchoring the decor. They also shine as spectacular accents and art pieces that instantaneously elevate a room’s look Post COVID, the focus is back on our homes, and homeowners have doubled down not only on the comfort factor but also on the luxury quotient. And that’s where carpets weave their magic.


Take a look at the 10 bestseller rugs of 2023

1. Someplace In Time

This hand-knotted rug is made of 100 percent wool. Its vintage look can add a dash of sophistication to any living room. Or better still, it can be showcased as a tapestry or a framed artwork on the wall. The collection is inspired by Afghan influences that recapture a lost era of raw grandeur and tenacious craftsmanship.
 

Traditionally designed, this light coral rug is a timeless piece that is perfect for a traditional
setting and modern home.

Shop This Rug


2. Aurora

This unique handmade artisanal rug is 48 percent wool and 52 percent silk, lavish with traditional motifs and designs. It's contrasting colors and striking patterns evoke a sense of nostalgia for an era gone by. The carpet has over 200 knots per square inch, a feat only accomplished by very skilled craftsmen.

The regal design of this classic Persian-style rug brings a sense of opulence to the space.

Shop This Rug


3. Hacienda - Light turquoise

This light turquoise floral rug made of pure wool bursts through like a heady spring across the Spanish countryside. Perfect as a piece de resistance in a pastel or neutral-colored living room, it is also available as a floral runner rug that can be used as a spectacular addition to the home’s entrance hallway. You can also use it as a colorful staircase landing carpet or a passageway runner rug.
 

This colorful carpet is made of 100 percent wool and has a medium pile thickness of 6-9mm.

Shop This Rug
 

The vibrancy of this multicolored floral area rug can energize a home’s decor and energy. With its fetching petals, buds, and paisley motifs, the red floral rug turns a muted, minimalist area into a gallery-esque space.
 

4. Revolution

This silver-gray hand-knotted rug is pure wool. The design combines distressed and vintage looks with traditional motifs. Perfect as a modern living room rug, this design is fit to be a family heirloom. You can pair it with neutral-coloured sofas and furnishings or place it in an Indian contemporary living room. Available in light grey, green, peacock blue, and orange, the collection offers options that will fit any type of decor.
 

This classic carpet design is all about understated elegance.

Shop This Rug

Note: Wool rugs are the most durable, flame-resistant, and natural humidifiers. If they are maintained carefully, they can last a very long time.


5. Mythos - Rust Gold

Mildly soft, hand-tufted, and 100 percent wool, this rust orange creation is part of a versatile range of area rugs that fit all types of budgets. Though traditional in design, you can see the magic come alive when juxtaposed with modern decor. Available in various colors, this handcrafted wool rug’s size can also be customized per one’s space.

The Persian-style rug commands all the attention in this entry foyer.

Shop This Rug

Great as an entrance lobby rug or as a statement office rug, Mythos injects vibrancy into any space it is placed in.


6. Mythos - Blue classic grey

This sophisticated, classic grey carpet is sublime in its design and is a best-selling carpet for a reason. Its sheen is unparalleled, and the rug brings a sense of luxury and grandeur wherever it is placed. Make a statement by using it as a formal living room rug, under the dining table, or opting for a customised hallway runner rug. This soft and plush wool carpet is striking, affordable, and very durable.

 

Handmade in India, this mildly soft hand-tufted carpet (Mythos) is traditional in design
with a high pile thickness of 10-12mm.

Shop This Rug


7. Hacienda - Aqua foam

Made from a blend of wool and viscose, this modern hand-tufted floral rug (in aqua foam colour) evokes a sense of luxury. Viscose mimics the feel of silk, while wool adds softness and sturdiness.

 

Available as 2x3 ft rugs or 8x8 ft carpets, their sizes can also be customized according to one’s space.

Shop This Rug

The cheerful floral patterns make it perfect as a study area rug or a living room carpet.
 

8. Genesis - Antique white

A white 8x10 ft area rug can almost always find pride in place in any living room. And when it is an abstract off-white area rug with pastel accents, then it becomes a must-buy. This elegant, modern rug is part wool, part viscose and can complement all types of decor, and comes in various sizes.

The Genesis range of carpets is affordable and durable. Available in many colours,
Each rug has a high pile thickness of 10-12mm.

Shop This Rug

This best-selling carpet design can heighten the pristine look of minimalist room decor or subdue the effects of an eclectic space. It is also ideal as a kids’ room rug or a plush addition to the family lounge. See more of the collection here.

 

9. Genesis - Classic gray

A soft, classic gray rug is a must-have home decor accessory. Genesis elevates a grey rug's chic quotient by adding swirl patterns of rocks as accents to the design. These rugs can be customized as per color and size and have a high pile thickness of 10-12 mm.

 

This hand-tufted rug’s design is all about a marbled effect.

Shop This Rug

This design with a marbled effect makes it a great flooring option as a rug on a laminated floor.


10. Aqua

The denim blue flat-weave rug evokes a boho chic vibe with its bold colors and straight lines. Made of pure wool and woven with the highest-quality fibers, this rug can be used in casual areas, both indoors and outdoors.

 

This carpet has a mildly coarse texture, perfect for breezy verandahs.

Shop This Rug

The versatility of this all-wool rug makes it a bestselling flat weave carpet design. When placed in an all-white or brown space, it can add a bit of a Moroccan mood or heighten the effect of coastal-themed or Bohemian-style decor.
